About LTS

The Literacy Training Service (LTS) is one of three components of the National Service Training Program,
a civic education and defense preparedness program for higher and vocational education students in the Philippines.
The LTS is "a program designed to train students to become teachers of literacy and numeracy skills to school children, out of school youth, and other segments of society"
